The Gray Man (2022)

The film stars Ryan Gosling, Chris Evans, Ana de Armas, Jessica Henwick, Wagner Moura, Julia Butters, Dhanush, Alfre Woodard and Billy Bob Thornton. Directed by Anthony and Joe Russo from a screenplay by Christopher Markus and Stephen McFeely. Film is the first film in a franchise based on Mark Greaney's Gray Man novels.
