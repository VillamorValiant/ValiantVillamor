Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald (2018)

Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald is a 2018 fantasy film. It is the second instalment in the Fantastic Beasts film series. Set in 1927, it follows Newt Scamander and Albus Dumbledore as they try to take down Gellert Grindlwald.
